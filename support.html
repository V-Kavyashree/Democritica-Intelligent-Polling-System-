Support

At Democratica, we prioritize your convenience and ensure that your voting experience is seamless and stress-free. If you encounter any issues or have any questions, our dedicated support team is here to help. Below, you'll find solutions to common problems and ways to contact us if further assistance is needed.

FAQs

  • How do I register to vote? Visit the Register page for a step-by-step guide.
  • What do I do if I forget my password? Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page to reset it.
  • Is my vote anonymous? Yes, all votes are encrypted and confidential.
  • Can I change my vote? No, once submitted, a vote cannot be altered to maintain integrity.

Contact Support

For further assistance, reach out to our support team via the Contact Us page. We are available 24/7 to address your concerns.
